# Psybershock
First person dungeon crawler inspired by Ace Attorney
* https://i.imgur.com/3DJUcF8.gif 
* https://i.imgur.com/TMuVjWa.gif

Controls:

W and S to move forward and backward.
A and D to turn left and right
Q and E To strafe left and right
F to Interact
R to 'Talk'
J to cancel conversations

Download by clicking the psybershock.console.7z . Extract it and run the Psybershock file, not the psybershock.console .
